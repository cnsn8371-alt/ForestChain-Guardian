# Forest Conservation Smart Contract Suite\n\n## Overview\n\nThis pull request introduces a comprehensive blockchain-based forest conservation platform consisting of four interconnected smart contracts built on the Stacks blockchain using Clarity. The system enables real-time monitoring, biodiversity tracking, sustainable logging verification, and tokenized conservation incentives.\n\n## Smart Contracts Implemented\n\n### 1. Deforestation Detection System (`deforestation-detection-system.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Real-time monitoring and alerting system for illegal logging and forest clearing activities.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **Sensor Network Management**: Register and manage IoT sensors across forest areas\n- **Alert System**: Submit, verify, and track deforestation alerts with threat level classification\n- **Geographic Tracking**: Precise latitude/longitude coordinate validation and storage\n- **Emergency Response**: Automatic emergency state detection based on area-at-risk thresholds\n- **Authorization Framework**: Role-based access control for operators and verifiers\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `register-sensor`: Deploy new monitoring sensors\n- `submit-alert`: Report deforestation activities with location and impact data\n- `update-alert-status`: Track alert resolution progress\n- `verify-alert`: Validate alerts through authorized personnel\n\n**Technical Specifications**:\n- **272+ lines of Clarity code**\n- **Threat levels**: Low, Medium, High, Critical\n- **Alert statuses**: Active, Investigating, Resolved, False Positive\n- **Emergency threshold**: Configurable area-at-risk monitoring\n\n### 2. Forest Biodiversity Registry (`forest-biodiversity-registry.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Comprehensive species population tracking and ecosystem health monitoring platform.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **Species Database**: Complete registry with scientific names, conservation status, and habitat information\n- **Monitoring Sites**: Establish and manage research locations with multiple monitoring methods\n- **Population Tracking**: Record and validate species population observations\n- **Conservation Status Management**: IUCN Red List compatible status tracking\n- **Ecosystem Health Assessment**: Biodiversity indices and habitat quality metrics\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `register-species`: Add new species to conservation database\n- `establish-monitoring-site`: Set up research locations with equipment tracking\n- `record-population`: Log species observations with confidence levels\n- `assess-ecosystem-health`: Evaluate overall environmental conditions\n\n**Technical Specifications**:\n- **408+ lines of Clarity code**\n- **Conservation statuses**: Least Concern through Extinct (7 levels)\n- **Monitoring methods**: Camera traps, acoustic sensors, visual surveys, tracking, satellite\n- **Research verification**: Authorized researcher network with institutional affiliations\n\n### 3. Sustainable Logging Verification (`sustainable-logging-verification.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Transparent certification and supply chain tracking for legal timber harvesting with mandatory replanting enforcement.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **Digital Permits**: Issue and manage logging permits with volume restrictions\n- **Certification System**: Support for FSC, PEFC, SFI, and custom certifications\n- **Chain of Custody**: Complete supply chain transparency from forest to market\n- **Replanting Enforcement**: Mandatory reforestation requirements with compliance tracking\n- **Harvest Verification**: Inspector-verified logging activities with batch tracking\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `issue-logging-permit`: Grant authorized harvesting rights\n- `issue-certification`: Provide sustainability certificates\n- `log-harvest`: Record harvesting activities with species and volume data\n- `record-replanting`: Track reforestation efforts with survival rates\n- `verify-harvest`: Inspector validation of logging activities\n\n**Technical Specifications**:\n- **479+ lines of Clarity code**\n- **Permit lifecycle**: Active, Suspended, Revoked, Completed, Expired\n- **Certification types**: 5 major international standards supported\n- **Compliance threshold**: 80% minimum replanting requirement\n- **Inspector network**: Specialized verification personnel\n\n### 4. Forest Stewardship Rewards (`forest-stewardship-rewards.clar`)\n\n**Purpose**: Tokenized incentive system for conservation activities with staking rewards and governance mechanisms.\n\n**Key Features**:\n- **SIP-010 Compatible Token**: ForestGuardian (FRST) token with 6 decimal places\n- **Activity-Based Rewards**: Different reward multipliers for various conservation activities\n- **Staking System**: Lock tokens for governance participation and yield generation\n- **Verification Network**: Authorized verifiers with reputation scoring\n- **Governance Framework**: Proposal and voting mechanisms for system upgrades\n\n**Core Functions**:\n- `report-activity`: Submit conservation activities for rewards\n- `verify-activity`: Validate submitted activities through authorized network\n- `claim-reward`: Redeem tokens for verified conservation work\n- `stake-tokens`: Lock tokens for governance and staking rewards\n- `unstake-tokens`: Withdraw staked tokens after lock period\n\n**Technical Specifications**:\n- **471+ lines of Clarity code**\n- **Activity types**: Tree planting, wildlife monitoring, fire prevention, indigenous support\n- **Reward multipliers**: 1-20% based on activity type and quantity\n- **Staking rewards**: 5% annual yield for locked tokens\n- **Token supply**: 1M FRST maximum with controlled minting\n\n## System Architecture\n\n###
